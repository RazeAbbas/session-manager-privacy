Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [17 Feb, 2025]

1. Introduction

Thank you for using the Session Manager Chrome Extension. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

2. Information We Collect

The extension collects and stores the following information:

  • Open tabs & URLs: To save and restore browsing sessions.
  • Recently closed tabs: To allow users to recover previous sessions.
  • Local storage: To keep session data on the user's device.

We do not collect personal information, store data on external servers, or share data with third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

The data collected is used exclusively for saving and restoring your browsing sessions. All information is stored locally on your device.

4. Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • Tabs: To access and save open tab information.
  • Storage: To store session data locally.
  • Sessions: To restore recently closed tabs.

5. Data Security

We do not transfer or sell any data. All session information remains on your local device.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be reflected on this page.
